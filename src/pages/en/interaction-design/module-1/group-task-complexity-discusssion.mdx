# Group Task

Break into groups of 3-4 people. Each group will must choose an object improve and re-design. You will have 5 minutes to present your findings to the class.

- Give your group a name
- Nominate a group leader who will report your findings to the class

## Ask yourself the following questions:

- As the user, what are my goals? What are the primary goals and what are secondary?
- What constraints does this product have?
- What limitations does the current design have?
- Are there opportunities for a more logical and intuitive design?
